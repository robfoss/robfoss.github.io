The Lion

For all of their roaring, growling, and ferociousness, lions are family animals and truly social in their own communities. They usually live in groups of 15 or more animals called prides.

Here are a few random facts about the lion:

  • Lions are the only cats that live in groups.
  • A group, or pride, can be up to 30 lions, depending on how much food and water is available.
  • A lion's roar can be heard up to eight kilometres away.
  • Female lions are the main hunters
